Privacy Policy for Better Gemini

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

Overview

Better Gemini ("the Extension") is a browser extension that enhances your Google Gemini experience with UI improvements and productivity features. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

In short: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or conversations.

Data Collection

What We DO NOT Collect

  • Conversations: We never read, store, or transmit your Gemini conversations
  • Personal Information: No names, emails, or identifying information
  • Usage Analytics: No tracking, analytics, or telemetry
  • Browsing History: No monitoring of your browsing activity
  • Credentials: No access to your Google account credentials

What We Store Locally

The Extension stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in storage API:

DataPurpose
Feature toggle preferencesRemember which features you've enabled/disabled
Default model selectionRemember your preferred Gemini model
UI preferencesRemember your layout and display preferences

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension or clearing browser data

Permissions Explained

PermissionWhy It's Needed
activeTabTo apply UI enhancements to the Gemini page you're viewing
scriptingTo inject the features (wider layout, shortcuts, etc.) into Gemini
storageTo save your preferences locally on your device
host_permissionsTo run only on Google Gemini pages

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not:

  • Connect to any third-party services
  • Include any advertising or tracking SDKs
  • Send data to any external servers
  • Use any analytics platforms

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security
  • No data transmission means no risk of interception
  • No server-side storage means no risk of data breaches on our end

Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
